## School Contest

### Description

This repository is dedicated to managing and promoting a school contest called the “Energy
Challenge”. The aim of the contest is to encourage planning and collaborative efforts
among students and schools to save energy. The contest is open to all types of schools.

### Objective

The main objective of this contest is to educate students about the importance of energy
conservation and motivate them to take active steps in reducing energy consumption in
their schools.

### Participation

Interested schools can enroll in the contest and will be provided with a set of guidelines
and resources to help them plan and implement energy-saving strategies.

## Evaluation

The evaluation will be based on the _amount of energy_ each school manages to save during
the contest period. The creativity and effectiveness of the implemented energy-saving
strategies will also be taken into account.

## Prizes

Prizes will be awarded to the schools that achieve the highest energy savings. Specific
details about the prizes will be announced closer to the contest date.
